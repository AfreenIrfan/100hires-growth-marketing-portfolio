# SaaS Talk with the Metrics Brothers (now: The Metrics Brothers)

## Hosts
- **Dave "CAC" Kellogg** — long-time B2B Marketing executive
- **Ray "Growth" Rike** — long-time B2B Sales executive

## Podcast Overview
Formerly known as *SaaS Talk with the Metrics Brothers*, now rebranded as *The Metrics Brothers*. Each episode runs ~20 minutes and covers one topic critical to B2B SaaS and AI-native companies — focusing on customer acquisition, retention, and expansion metrics.

**Where to listen:** Spotify, Apple Podcasts

---

## Episodes Referenced

### Episode: Marketing & Sales Alignment
- CAC and Growth tackle why alignment between Sales and Marketing remains one of the top pipeline challenges in B2B SaaS
- Key topics: What alignment actually means vs what it isn't, how metrics can bridge the gap, top 3 actionable alignment ideas
- Relevant insight: Most GTM failures aren't strategy failures — they're alignment failures between two teams that share revenue goals but operate in silos

### Episode: 2025 State of B2B GTM (100th Episode)
- Based on a survey of 195 GTM leaders
- Key finding: Scaling existing GTM motions is the #1 priority, even when those motions are underperforming
- A "distraction chart" (Slide 12 of the report) showed B2B companies trying too many channels at once — the conclusion: less is more when it comes to content and channel strategy
- Relevant for LinkedIn content: Doing one channel extremely well beats doing many channels averagely

### Episode: B2B SaaS Pricing Benchmarks
- 67% of SaaS companies have introduced at least one element of Usage-Based Pricing (UBP)
- Content tied to pricing education drives high-intent engagement on LinkedIn — this is a topic SaaS buyers actively search

### Episode: Pipeline Efficiency Metrics
- Covers Cost per Opportunity and Pipe to Spend — two critical metrics to understand how much you need to invest to generate pipeline
- Relevant for content: LinkedIn content that educates on these metrics positions a SaaS brand as a thought leader for CFOs and RevOps leaders

## Key Ideas (Relevant to LinkedIn Organic Content for B2B SaaS)

- **Metrics-first storytelling:** B2B audiences on LinkedIn don't just want tips — they want benchmarks and data. CAC and Growth have built a following by grounding every opinion in a metric.
- **Niche consistency wins:** A 20-minute podcast that covers *one metric per episode* is more valuable than a broader show. Same logic applies to LinkedIn — one clear idea per post beats a listicle.
- **GTM alignment as a content pillar:** Marketing-Sales alignment is a perennial pain point in B2B SaaS. Content addressing this theme consistently performs well because it speaks to both audiences.
- **Pipeline crisis framing:** The idea that "nothing works in GTM anymore" is actively discussed in this community. Creating LinkedIn content that challenges or confirms that narrative taps into a live conversation.

## Why This Podcast Matters for LinkedIn Organic Strategy

SaaS Talk / The Metrics Brothers represents what a *metrics-native content voice* looks like. The key learning for B2B SaaS LinkedIn creators:

1. **Own a metric, own a conversation** — Dave owns "CAC," Ray owns "Growth." On LinkedIn, owning a specific lens (growth, retention, pipeline, etc.) makes your content instantly recognizable.
2. **Short, dense, and actionable** — 20-minute episodes. Same principle applies to LinkedIn posts: tight, insight-packed, no fluff.
3. **Data is your moat** — Referencing actual benchmark reports and surveys in content builds credibility faster than opinion pieces alone.
