## Key Ideas (Relevant to LinkedIn Organic Content for B2B SaaS)

- **The content flywheel is the strategy:** Great LinkedIn content is not the output — it's the input for every other content format. LinkedIn → Newsletter → Community → Event → Podcast. Each layer deepens audience relationships.
- **Personal > Company page, always:** This is the most consistent finding across all Exit Five content on LinkedIn. Human voices, even from small companies, outperform brand pages. For B2B SaaS, executives and employees posting is more valuable than the company page posting.
- **Post every day during the week, once per idea:** Dave's workflow is disciplined — post once a day, Monday–Friday, never duplicate ideas in the same day. Have a "content bank" of ideas waiting.
- **LinkedIn as a non-paid pipeline channel:** Dave explicitly frames LinkedIn as a channel that doesn't cost money — just time and consistency. For early-stage SaaS with lean budgets, this is the play.
- **Thought Leader Ads = organic with fuel:** Once you have posts performing organically, boosting them via Thought Leader Ads adds reach without killing authenticity.
- **Attention is the real game:** Dave's recent episodes focus on this — in a noisy B2B market, brands that earn attention (not buy it) win. Being boring on LinkedIn is the biggest mistake.
