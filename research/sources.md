## Research Methodology

All experts were identified and verified manually 
by the researcher on April 22, 2026. LinkedIn 
profiles and YouTube channels were reviewed 
directly via screenshots. Podcast experts were 
identified through TopRank B2B Marketing's curated 
list "10 Podcasts to Elevate Your B2B Marketing 
in 2025." Research was conducted with guidance 
from Claude AI for expert selection and description 
structuring. All facts mentioned are based only 
on what was directly visible and verified.

---

## Expert Selection Criteria

1. They actively post or create content themselves
   — not just write about it theoretically
2. They work specifically with B2B or SaaS companies
3. They share data, results and frameworks — 
   not just opinions or motivation
4. Minimum 20K followers for LinkedIn experts
   to ensure credibility signals

Generic names and first Google results were 
deliberately avoided. Every expert was manually 
verified by checking their profile, recent content, 
engagement and relevance to B2B SaaS LinkedIn 
strategy specifically.
