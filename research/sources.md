**Researcher:** Afreen Irfan

## Why I Chose This Topic

LinkedIn is the #1 platform for B2B SaaS companies 
to build authority, generate inbound leads, and grow 
without paid ads. Understanding how top practitioners 
execute organic content strategy on LinkedIn is 
directly applicable to growth marketing work at 
100Hires — an ATS SaaS product targeting businesses 
and HR teams.

---

## Expert Selection Criteria

I selected experts based on three filters:
1. They actively POST on LinkedIn themselves 
   (not just write about it)
2. They work specifically with B2B SaaS companies
3. They share data, results, and frameworks — 
   not just opinions
